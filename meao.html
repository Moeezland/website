About MEAO

MEAO serves as Moeezland’s dedicated platform for aeronautical experimentation and innovation. The Office focuses on research, demonstration, and symbolic aviation projects designed to promote technological curiosity, responsible development, and public awareness.

MEAO maintains a strictly non-combat and non-offensive mandate. All activities are conducted with safety, transparency, and restraint as guiding principles.

Facilities & Demonstrations

The Office conducts limited experimental and demonstration activities for educational and ceremonial purposes.

MEAO-X1 “Moeezland F-19”

The Moeezland F-19, internally designated as MEAO-X1, is an experimental demonstration platform operated under MEAO supervision.

The platform is manually controlled and used exclusively for experimental flight demonstrations and symbolic displays. It does not possess autonomous or weaponized capabilities.
