Archival Notice

Between 2019 and 2022, the Government of Moeezland did not issue public press releases. This period followed the foundation of the state and the conclusion of the Cold War with micronation of Hazistan, during which national efforts were focused on internal organization, legal groundwork, and long-term institutional planning. Official public communications resumed in end of 2022 with the drafting and adoption of Moeezland’s first Constitution.

Published: 23 July 2019

Micronation of Moeezland was founded

The Government of Moeezland formally recognizes 23 July 2019 as the date of the foundation of the State of Moeezland. At the time of its establishment, Moeezland operated without a singular written declaration of foundation, relying instead on practical assertion of sovereignty and early administrative actions. This recognition affirms the continuity of the state from its foundation in 2019 through subsequent constitutional and institutional development, including the adoption of the Constitution in 2022.
